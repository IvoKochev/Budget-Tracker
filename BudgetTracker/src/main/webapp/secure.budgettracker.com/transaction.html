My Transactions Questions

close view full page

Page Description

This page will show all your transactions by account and give you a running balance for the month starting from $0.00, as well as give you a total balance. To view your transactions by Payee, you can click the View Payee button below. To view your transactions by Budget Subcategory, click My Budget from the tab navigation above and select the name of the Budget Subcategory you want to view transactions for.

Terms

Reconciled
This means that your transaction has went through the bank and has already shown up on your monthly statement.
Void
This is for when you cancel a check or a transaction was cancellled by your bank.
Unreconciled
This means that the transaction has been made but either the bank doesn't know about it or it's pending.
Cleared
This means that the transaction has cleared your bank but hasn't shown up yet in your monthly statement.

Questions & Answers

Why aren't my transactions showing up?
First check that your filters are all cleared in the transaction filter section at the top. Then change the account selection to combined accounts. This will show you all transactions for a give date range. You can then edit the transaction to see which account it belongs to.
Is there an easier way to search for a specific transaction?
Yes, at the top of the page you should see a "Transaction Search" button. This will allow you to enter in a partial payee name and will return all matches for that search.
What does "round up" do?
Let's say you that every time you spent money you rounded it up to the nearest dollar. Ever want to see how much you'd have saved if you did so? Well this will show how much you'd have left over if you deposited alll your extra change to a savings account.
Why doesn't my balance match my bank?
First change your date range to show all and select the account in question. You can see from the beginning where the balance was thrown off in your running balance. If you have several transactions, instead you can click the balance account link. This will allow you to either adjust your starting balance to match or create a new "Balance Account" transaction to bring it up to date.
What does download transaction refer to?
Download Transactions allows you to export your transactions to an excel spreadsheet or CSV file.
How do you record a individual credit card transaction? When I put in a cc transaction and select the Withdrawal/Charge it deducts from the balance and when I select Deposit/Credit it adds to the balance (which I want it to do). The problem I run into is when I make a cc payment through Bill Pay/transfer it treats that as a Deposit/Credit which adds to my balance not subtract it which is what you expect a cc payment to do. I'm very confused on how to handle cc transactions.
For the credit card transfer, your From account should be your Checking and your To Account should be your Credit Card. This will ensure the deposit amount reduces your credit card debt. The other possible problem is if you are starting with a positive balance. Make sure your credit card balance is either negative or 0.
how do i reconcile transactions
When editing a transaction, you should see the option to mark it as reconciled. If not, go to settings and make sure you have the ability to mark cleared under transactions
how do you reconcile bank statement
To reconcile transactions, you would go to My Transactions and click the orange U next to the transaction to switch to Cleared and R for reconciled
can i split a transaction
Yes, when you hover over a transaction, you can choose the split transaction option. Alternatively, if you edit a transaction, next to the amount you should see a split transactions link.
