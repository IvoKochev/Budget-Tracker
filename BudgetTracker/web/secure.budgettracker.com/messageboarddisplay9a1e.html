Updating your bank account amounts
When editing your account on the My Accounts page, instead of seeing a field to change the opening balance, we've replaced this with your current balance. This change was also applied to the mobile site for a quick way to balance your account.

Issue Reporting
We've added a new channel to report issues found on the site. At the top right of the screen you'll now see "Report Issue" where you can specify if it's a bug, new idea, or just a general question. Then at the bottom of each page you'll see a "My Reported Issues" link. While you can continue to use the Message Boards for issues, if you have data sensitive information to help us track down the issue, you can use this way of communicating with us instead. Additionally, you'll also see an option for the status of the issue so you can see if it's been resolved or not. Comments will continue to be communicated through email but this will give a single place to check on if your issue was addressed or not.

Gift Cards
We've added a new type of account called Gift Cards to the My Accounts page. This will get added to your Net Worth. In addition, we've also added a Gift Card section under More... to the mobile website. While the current accounts prevent only the last 4 of your account number, the gift card accounts will allow you to enter in the full gift card number so you can now have the number with you on your mobile device in case you leave your gift cards at home. You'll also be able to track how much is left on your gift cards.

Balance Account Updates
We've updated the Balance Account option to make it easier to understand. Instead of forcing you to clear all transactions to bring your account balance up to date, we've change the checkbox to allow you to state if you want to include unreconciled transactions or not. By leaving this checked, your balance will be adjusted to the amount you enter. Additionally, if you are updating your balance on the My Accounts page and you want to adjust your balance for a future date, just change the "as of" date at the top of the page and this will get reflected as of the date you choose instead of the current date.
